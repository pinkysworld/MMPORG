Frontier Valley

Inspired by the Aventurian tales of Sternenschweif, wander the Svellt Valley, befriend its creatures, and gather legendary gear for your party.

    Adventuring Tips

    • Legendary beasts echo the bestiary of Sternenschweif. Offer patience, then claim their tribute.
    • The valley's slopes hide herbs and minerals for future crafting systems.
    • Watch the skies and weather; dusk light guides you back to camp.
